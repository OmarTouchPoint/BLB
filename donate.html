Thank you for considering a donation to Big Little Bows. Your contribution will directly benefit LGBTQ+ teens struggling with substance use disorder and mental health challenges.

Every donation, no matter how small, makes a difference in the lives of our Little Bows.

How You Can Help

Monetary Donation

If you're able to donate monetarily, please take a moment to contribute to our Big Little Bow Fund. Your donation will support our programs and initiatives, providing essential resources and support to LGBTQ+ teens in need.
DONATE NOW

Spread the Word

If you're unable to donate monetarily, that's okay! You can still make a difference by sharing our page with your friends, family, and social networks. Help us reach more people who can support our mission and make a positive impact in the lives of LGBTQ+ teens.

Donate Your Time

  • Consider donating your time and join our program as a volunteer Big Bow. By becoming a mentor to our Little Bows, you can provide invaluable support and guidance to young people in need.
  • Your time and expertise can make a lasting difference in the lives of LGBTQ+ teens struggling with substance use disorder and mental health challenges.

Where Your Donation Goes

The funds we collect directly benefit our Little Bows, providing them with the support, resources, and opportunities they need to achieve sobriety and mental wellness. Additionally, your donation helps support Little Bows who have aged out of our program and are now 18+, providing financial assistance for LGBTQ+ affirming sober livings and treatment centers.
No one should be denied access to affirming care due to financial barriers. We understand finding affirming options that specialize in the unique needs of the LGBTQ+ community can be challenging which is why we have partnered with verified programs that specialize in affirming care.

Your donations help fund our mentorship programs, allowing us to match Little Bows with supportive and understanding Big Bow mentors who share their gender identity, expression, and orientation.

With your generous support, we can continue to provide life- changing resources and opportunities for LGBTQ+ teens on their journey to recovery and wellness. Thank you for being a vital part of our mission to be someone's light at the end of the tunnel.
