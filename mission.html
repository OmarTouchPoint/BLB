At Big Little Bows, our mission is to provide unwavering support, guidance, and hope to LGBTQ+ teens between the ages of 15-17 who are struggling with substance use disorder and mental health challenges. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of gender identity, expression, or orientation.
Empowering LGBTQ+ Teens

We are dedicated to empowering LGBTQ+ teens to overcome adversity and achieve lasting sobriety and mental wellness.

Our programs and initiatives are designed to create a safe and supportive environment where young individuals can feel understood, accepted, and valued for who they are.

Providing Mentorship and Support

Central to our mission is the provision of mentorship and support from our team of Big Bows, who serve as role models and companions to our Little Bows.

Each Little Bow is matched with a Big Bow who shares their gender identity, expression, and orientation, ensuring that they receive personalized guidance and understanding throughout their journey.

Fostering Resilience and Recovery

We are committed to fostering resilience and recovery among LGBTQ+ youth by equipping them with the necessary tools, resources, and coping strategies to overcome challenges and build a brighter future. Through educational workshops, community support, and individualized guidance, we empower our Little Bows to make positive choices and embrace their true selves.

Promoting Access to Affirming Aftercare

At Big Little Bows, we hold firm to the belief that access to affirming aftercare is not just beneficial, but absolutely essential for the long-term success and well-being of LGBTQ+ individuals in recovery.

That's why we passionately advocate for and provide crucial financial assistance to ensure that all our Little Bows, whether currently minors or having aged out of our program, have access to LGBTQ+ affirming sober livings and treatment centers.

Spreading Hope and Inspiration


Through our work, we aim to spread hope and inspiration to LGBTQ+ teens across the country, showing them that recovery is possible and that they are not alone in their journey. By sharing stories of resilience, courage, and triumph, we hope to inspire others to seek help, embrace support, and believe in their ability to overcome challenges.

Join us at Big Little Bows in our mission to empower LGBTQ+ teens, foster resilience and recovery, and spread hope and inspiration to individuals in need. Together, we can create a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Becoming a Big Bow

Are you passionate about making a difference in the lives of LGBTQ+ teens struggling with substance use disorder and mental health challenges? Consider becoming a Big Bow and joining our team of dedicated mentors. As a Big Bow, you will have the opportunity to provide guidance, support, and encouragement to a young person in need.

Qualifications:
  • Personal experience overcoming challenges with substances, gender identity, and being a proud member of the LGBTQ+ Community
  • Minimum of two (2) years of sobriety
  • Compassionate and empathetic attitude
  • Ability to actively listen and provide non-judgmental support
  • Commitment to the well-being and success of our Little Bows
