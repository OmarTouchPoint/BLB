About Us

Big Little Bows is a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting LGBTQ+ teens ages 15-17 who are on the journey to recovery from substance use disorder. We understand that adolescence can be a challenging time, particularly for young individuals who are navigating their gender identity, orientation, and recovery simultaneously. 

That's why we provide a safe and supportive space where teens, whether they identify as gay, bi, trans, nonbinary, or questioning, can find guidance and mentorship from our team of Big Bows, who have walked a similar path. Our mission is to show every Little Bow that recovery is possible, and they can achieve the life they've always dreamed of. With the support of our community, we empower teens to embrace their true selves and build a brighter future.

Our Mission

Empowering LGBTQ+ teens on the path to recovery, Big Little Bows is dedicated to providing unwavering support, guidance, and hope. Through mentorship, advocacy, and community, we inspire our Little Bows to embrace their true selves, overcome challenges, and believe in the possibility of a brighter future.

Together, we show that recovery is not only achievable, but a journey filled with endless potential and boundless opportunities. Our motto, "Be someone's light at the end of the tunnel," encapsulates our commitment to being a beacon of hope and support for every individual we serve.


Our Approach

Mentorship

Our Big Bows are individuals who have successfully overcome similar challenges with substances, gender identity, and orientation.

They serve as mentors and companions to our Little Bows, offering empathetic support, guidance, and encouragement throughout their journey to sobriety and mental wellness.
Big Bow Mentors

At Big Little Bows, we provide a safe and non-judgmental space for our Little Bows to share their struggles and fears.

Our Big Bows offer unconditional support and understanding, helping our Little Bows see the light at the end of the tunnel and empowering them to overcome obstacles.
Tools for Lasting Sobriety

We equip our Little Bows with the necessary tools and resources to achieve lasting sobriety and mental wellness.

Through personalized guidance, educational workshops, and community support, we empower our Little Bows to make positive choices and build a bright future for themselves.

Becoming a Big Bow

Are you passionate about making a difference in the lives of LGBTQ+ teens struggling with substance use disorder and mental health challenges? Consider becoming a Big Bow and joining our team of dedicated mentors. As a Big Bow, you will have the opportunity to provide guidance, support, and encouragement to a young person in need.

Qualifications:
  • Personal experience overcoming challenges with substances, gender identity, and being a proud member of the LGBTQ+ Community
  • Minimum of two (2) years of sobriety
  • Compassionate and empathetic attitude
  • Ability to actively listen and provide non-judgmental support
  • Commitment to the well-being and success of our Little Bows
