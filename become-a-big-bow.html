Youth who struggle with substance abuse can feel disconnected from others, especially if they also experience gender identity or sexual orientation issues.

Becoming a Big Bow can create a meaningful connection with a person on the path to recovery and become an integral part of their recovery process.

If you’re a member of the LGBTQIA+ community who has successfully achieved and maintained your sobriety, has strong values, and is willing to share your experience and donate your time, please consider becoming a Big Bow recovery mentor.
