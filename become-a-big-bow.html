A Big Bow at Big Little Bows is more than just a mentorâ€”they are a guiding light for LGBTQ+ teens navigating the challenges of substance use disorder and mental health issues. Each Big Bow is a compassionate individual who has personally overcome addiction and is committed to sharing their experience, strength, and hope with the next generation.

Through personalized support, empathy, and understanding, Big Bows offer invaluable guidance and mentorship, helping our Little Bows see the possibilities of recovery and the fulfillment of their dreams. They serve as role models, advocates, and champions for the resilience and potential of every LGBTQ+ teen they mentor, embodying our mission to be someone's light at the end of the tunnel.

Meet Our Big Bows

Responsibilities

  • Serve as a mentor and role model to a Little Bow within the LGBTQ+ community
  • Provide emotional support, guidance, and encouragement
  • Help your Little Bow develop coping skills and strategies for sobriety and mental wellness
  • Attend training sessions and workshops to enhance your mentoring skills

How to Apply

If you're interested in becoming a Big Bow, please fill out our volunteer application form here. Once we receive your application, a member of our team will reach out to you to discuss next steps.
